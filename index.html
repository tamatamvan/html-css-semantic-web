About Us

  • Who We Are
  • Vision & Mission
  • Core Value

In this fast changing lifestyle, traditional food and beverage are becoming pushed aside in the market. People tend to choose modern products with fresh and attractive looks.

Back in 2006, Susan’s Food had an idea to bridge the traditional products with the opportunity in modern market. We started to develop a better production method, packing process, and a more appealing packaging design.


VISION

Bringing traditional products to be able to compete with modern products.

MISSION

1. To develop the production process in compliance with good manufacturing standards.
2. To expand the market of tradtional products into various distribution channels.
3. To develop a better and more attractive product appearance.


Creativity & Innovation


combined with standardized production method to create competitive products.

FOUNDER'S THOUGHT

Traditional delicacies are treasure to preserve
